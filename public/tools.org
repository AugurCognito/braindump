** =tcpdump=
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       0aedaeef-5edf-4e19-a3ae-3220c6713f15
:END:
*Use:* Capture and filters TCP/IP packets over a network/interface or file.

*Usage*
#+begin_src shell
tcpdump -i [interface]
#+end_src
To list all interfaces
#+begin_src shell
tcpdump -D
#+end_src
More detail at
#+begin_src shell
man tcpdump
#+end_src
